23 Free Geometric, Angular, Rune-esque Style Fonts

These angular and geometric style fonts have become increasing popular over the last couple years. They immediately bring a sense of mysticism to any design project because of their tinge of strangeness. But that’s what makes them unique.  They remind one of ancient runes or hieroglyphs. In todays design they emote an experimental and futuristic feeling.

All of these fonts are free and can be found by clicking on any of the graphics below.  They were all found via Behance.net.  I’ve linked to the Behance pages because I know the authors would like to control the download links for their own designs and they could change in the future.  Updated 4/3/2015
